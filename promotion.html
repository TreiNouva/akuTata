Frequently Asked Questions

Can I combine multiple promotional offers?
Unfortunately, most promotional offers cannot be combined with other ongoing promotions unless specifically stated. Each promotion has its own terms and conditions that will be clearly stated on the offer details.
How do I apply a promo code?
You can apply a promo code during the checkout process. After adding products to your cart, proceed to checkout where you'll find a field to enter your promo code. Once entered, click "Apply" and the discount will be automatically calculated.
When will I receive my cashback?
Cashback is typically processed within 7-14 business days after your order has been delivered and confirmed. The cashback amount will be credited to your V-Phone account which can be used for future purchases.
Are promotions valid for international shipping?
Most promotions are valid for domestic orders within Indonesia. For international shipping, promotional offers may vary. Please check the specific terms and conditions of each promotion or contact our customer service for more information.
